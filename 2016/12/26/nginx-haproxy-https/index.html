+------+  +------+  +------+

各软件作用:

  • Keepalived: 判断HaProxy存活,保证HA
  • Haproxy: 做Http Load Balance
  • Nginx(ssl): 与Haproxy放置同一台服务器,负责ssl offload
  • Nginx(LB): load balancer for app&web server
